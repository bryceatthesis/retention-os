## 30/60/90 Plan Implications

The referenced template was built around brand immersion, program takeover,
operational audit, and a pitch-deck-style deliverable. For retention, adapt the
shape as:

| Period | Focus | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| Days 1-30 | Learn systems, people, data, and live lifecycle state. | Retention source-of-truth map, Klaviyo audit, first cohort readout. |
| Days 31-60 | Prioritize fixes and ship low-risk improvements. | Flow hygiene plan, dunning/winback/onboarding recommendations, test roadmap. |
| Days 61-90 | Move from diagnosis to operating cadence. | Retention scorecard, experiment calendar, ownership model, and first strategic bet. |
