## Early Strategic Themes

1. Retention needs a current source-of-truth layer: metrics, source systems, flow inventory, segment definitions, and decision rules.
2. Klaviyo hygiene is likely a prerequisite to speed: account clutter, stale lists, duplicate/dead metrics, and legacy flows make confident action harder.
3. The retention problem is structural, not just campaign-level: cancellation volume was persistent across acquisition swings in the historical audit.
4. Lifecycle SMS appears underdeveloped relative to email and should be rebuilt carefully because prior SMS promo sends showed list-damage risk.
5. Product-specific lifecycle paths matter: Thesis, Stasis, kids products, OTP buyers, and reactivated subscribers likely need different journeys.
6. AI leverage is expected, but the durable asset is the operating system: documented definitions, playbooks, reusable queries, and review cadences.
